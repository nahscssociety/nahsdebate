About Our Debate Team

Developing tomorrow's leaders through the art of debate

North Atlanta High School Debate Team is dedicated to cultivating critical thinking, effective communication, and a passion for debate among students. Founded in 2010, our award-winning team has grown to become one of the most respected debate programs in the region.

We provide a supportive environment where students can develop confidence, research skills, and logical reasoning while competing in tournaments across the state and nation.

Debate Formats

Explore the different styles of debate we compete in

Policy Debate

A research-intensive format focusing on policy proposals where teams argue for and against implementing specific plans. Develops in-depth research and analytical skills.

  • Critical analysis
  • Research skills
  • Speaking at speed

Lincoln-Douglas Debate

One-on-one debates focusing on logic, ethical values, and philosophy. Named after the famous debates between Abraham Lincoln and Stephen Douglas.

  • Moral reasoning
  • Philosophical frameworks
  • Persuasive speaking

Public Forum Debate

Team debate format focusing on current events and public policy issues. Designed to be accessible and appeal to a wide audience.

  • Clarity and accessibility
  • Current events knowledge
  • Teamwork

World Schools Debate

Combines prepared topics with impromptu debates, emphasizing rhetorical skills and teamwork with elements of both policy and values debate.

  • International perspective
  • Impromptu speaking
  • Team strategy

Speech

Students present speeches about various topics.

  • International perspective
  • Impromptu speaking
  • Team strategy

Congressional Debate

A mock legislative assembly where students play the role of legislators by drafting and arguing over bills.

  • International perspective
  • Impromptu speaking
  • Team strategy
